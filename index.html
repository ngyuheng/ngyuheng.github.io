Research

A selection of some of my research work across various fields of study.

Synthesis and Properties of Chitin-Derived Hydrogels

This research focuses on the synthesis and characterization of a hydrogel derived from chitin which was obtained from crabs. The hydrogel's ability to adsorb heavy metal ions and various dyes from water was also investigated.

Organic Chemistry Environmental Science

Optimised Numerical Methods for the Computation of Particles in Fusion Hotspots and Particle Interactions in Magnetized Liner Inertial Fusion (MagLIF)

This project proposes a novel approach to optimising the computation of particle interactions in fusion hotspots and in Magnetized Liner Inertial Fusion (MagLIF). Using the Barnes Hut Algorithm, a more efficient method for simulating these interactions was introduced.

Plasma Physics Particle Physics Computational Physics

Synthesising and Comparing the Microplastic Degradation Efficiencies of Magnetically Removable Bismuth Based Microswimmers

This research focuses on a modified synthesis and subsequent characterization of magnetically removable self-propelling BiVO4 and Bi2WO6 microswimmers and their efficiency in degrading microplastics via Advanced Oxidative Processes.

Materials Science Environmental Science Inorganic Chemistry

Optimisation of Continuous Development from Flatsat to 3D Cubesat – A Novel Over-the-Air Solution

This research validates the use of Over-The-Air (OTA) reprogramming and debugging to eliminate the need to deconstruct working hardware, thus eliminating points of failure, and significantly decreasing development time while keeping costs low.

Electrical Engineering Software Engineering Embedded Systems
